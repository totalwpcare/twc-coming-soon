== Installation ==

= From your WordPress dashboard =
1. Navigate to `Plugins > Add New` from your WP Admin dashboard.
2. Search for `TWC Coming Soon Page, Under Construction & Maintenance Mode`.
3. Install `TWC Coming Soon Page, Under Construction & Maintenance Mode` and Activate the plugin.

= From WordPress.org =
1. Search for `TWC Coming Soon Page, Under Construction & Maintenance Mode` and download it.
2. Unzip and upload the `TWC Coming Soon Page, Under Construction & Maintenance Mode` directory to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
3. Activate `TWC Coming Soon Page, Under Construction & Maintenance Mode` from the Plugins tab of your admin dashboard.
